About Flawless Captures...

I photograph weddings & elopements. It's what I do, and it's what I love!

Hi, I'm Pranav! I am originally from Somnath and have grown my busines service area to India over the last 5 years. I am a people person, and enjoy working closely with couples, and capturing the essence of who they are. When it comes to photographing a couple, I look past the outward appearance for the love and emotion that drives them. The personalities that are distinct to each couple make every session new and unique. I strive to create a fun and spontaneous atmosphere and a collection of images that convey each moment, and every detail in an artistic and truthful manner. Through my many years of experience photographing weddings, I have developed a creative process that will help tell the story of who you are as a couple and what makes this the most important moment in your lives.

Moreover, I capture various types of photography,like Personal Potraits, Travel Destinations,Products, Videos, etc. I have even participated in various events across India, specially in Gujarat.Among which Kakariya Karnival, Patang Ustav, etc. are my favourite.
